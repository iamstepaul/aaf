Ayomide Ajiboye Scholarship FUL (AAS FUL)

Ayomide Ayomide is an alumni of Federal University Lokoja (FUL). He graduated from the Department of Mathematical Sciences in 2018 as the best graduating student. He is currently pursuing his graduate studies in Mathematics in the United States. See more.

Ayomide Ajiboye through Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation (AAF) offers Scholarships to final year students in Faculty of Science, Federal University Lokoja (FUL).

Eligibility:

  1. Be a Registered FULL TIME undergraduate in Federal University Lokoja.
  2. Be a certified 400 level student at the time of application.
  3. Show proof of completion of 300 level (100-300 transcripts).
  4. Show proof of academic motivation ( Statement of Purpose, CV).
  5. Show proof of academic testimonial (Letters of Recommendation)

The scholarship award is in two forms:

  1. A cash prize of fifty thousand naira (N 50,000 naira) to the best two (2) applicant only.
  2. A free two-month United States (US) scholarship application mentorship (Graduate school applications) to the best ten (10) applicants (starts in July).
  3. Show proof of completion of 300 level (100-300 transcripts).
  4. Show proof of academic motivation ( Statement of Purpose, CV).
  5. Show proof of academic testimonial (Letters of Recommendation)

Give details in your application to stand a better chance.
