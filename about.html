- About Us -

we empower and inspire future generations

Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation (AAF) strives to empower and inspire future generations by providing access to educational opportunities. We believe that every individual has the potential to make a meaningful impact on their communities and the world, and we are committed to helping them achieve their dreams.


Through our scholarship program, we aim to remove financial barriers that may prevent talented and deserving students from pursuing higher education. We believe that by investing in the education of our youth, we are investing in a brighter future for all.

We are dedicated to creating a common ground while promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in our scholarship selection process. We believe that everyone should have equal opportunities to succeed, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our foundation is built on the values of excellence, integrity, and compassion. We are committed to providing the highest quality of service to our scholarship recipients, donors, and partners. We are accountable for our actions and continuously strive for improvement.

At Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation (AAF), we envision a world where education is accessible to all, and every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential. We believe that together, we can make a positive impact on the lives of our students and their communities.
