The Past Events and Experiences that shape our present and future

Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation (AAF) was established in March 2023 with the vision of creating a world where every student has the opportunity to achieve their full potential through education. Our founder, Ayomide Ajiboye, was passionate about breaking down the barriers that prevent talented and deserving students from pursuing higher education. He envisioned a scholarship foundation that would empower and inspire future generations, and provide access to educational opportunities for all.

Since our inception, we have not awarded any scholarships. In future, we hope to offer numerous scholarships to students from diverse backgrounds, helping them to pursue their dreams and make a positive impact on their communities.

We are looking forward to partnering with various organizations and other institutions to promote diversity, equity, and inclusion in our scholarship selection process.

As we continue to grow and expand our reach, we remain committed to our core values of excellence, integrity, and compassion. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality of service to our scholarship recipients, donors, and partners, and to creating a brighter future for all through education.
