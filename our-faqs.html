The mission of the scholarship foundation is to provide financial assistance to deserving students in order to help them pursue their educational goals and achieve their full potential.

Eligibility criteria for scholarship applicants will be determined by the foundation's board of directors and will be based on factors such as financial need, academic achievement, and community involvement.

Application information and deadlines will be available on the foundation's website. Applicants will need to submit relevant documents and complete an application form to be considered for a scholarship.

Scholarship recipients are selected by the foundation's board of directors based on a thorough review of each applicant's academic and personal achievements, financial need, and potential for success.
